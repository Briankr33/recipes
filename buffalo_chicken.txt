Ingredients
4 chicken breasts , boneless, skinless
4 oz cream cheese , softened
2 tablespoons Franks Red Hot Wings Buffalo Sauce (I used the mild one)
6 strips bacon , cooked
1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese , shredded

Instructions
Preheat oven to 375 F.

Place chicken breasts in a casserole dish. 

Mix softened cream cheese with Buffalo Sauce. Spread over chicken breasts. 

Top with chopped, cooked bacon. 

Top with shredded Mozzarella cheese. 

Bake in the preheated oven at 375 F for 20-25 minutes until chicken is done. The cooking time depends on the thickness of your chicken breasts.
